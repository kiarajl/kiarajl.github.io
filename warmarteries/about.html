What's a zine?

Zine: A noncommercial often homemade or online publication usually devoted to specialized and often unconventional subject matter

What is warm arteries?

Warm arteries is an art zine. A collection of drawings made by us that we want to share with you! There isn't much of a set theme or anything, it's just random art that we've created that we think other people would like to see :)

Who are we?

Our names are Lizzie England and Kiara Larrabee. We're two students who like to draw in our free time. We thought our drawing styles went well together and decided a zine would be a cool way to display them together.
